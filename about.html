About Us

About Lockwood Construction

Lockwood Construction, LLC has been a trusted name in home exterior improvements for over a decade. Serving the Twin Cities Metro Area, we specialize in roofing, siding, windows, and gutters. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service.

We take pride in transforming homes and ensuring our clients are satisfied with the results. Whether you need a small repair or a complete exterior renovation, Lockwood Construction is here to help.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality home improvement services that enhance the beauty, value, and functionality of your home. We strive to exceed customer expectations through professionalism, integrity, and attention to detail.
